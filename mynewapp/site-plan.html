Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to simplify the process of finding applications that help with specific aspects of students' life.

Audience

The students of BYU Idaho

Dynamic elements

My plan to involve Javascript to the site is to filter into categories the applications. This would include using a list with at least two categories, when users choose one category, with Javascript, the site will answer by showing the respective content and hiding the one for the other category.
